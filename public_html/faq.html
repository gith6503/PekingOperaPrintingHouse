How do I submit a print job?

You can submit your print job by visiting the Submit Print Job page and uploading your file along with the necessary specifications.


Can I track the progress of my print job?

Yes, after submitting your print job, you can track its status by visiting the Track Job page.


How can I contact Peking Opera Printing House?

You can contact us through email, Facebook, or WhatsApp call. All contact details are available on the Contact Us page. You can also send a message directly through our website.


What file types are accepted for printing?

We accept files in .jpg, .png, and .pdf formats. Please ensure your file meets these requirements before submitting your print job.


How do I top up my balance for printing?

You can top up your balance by visiting the Top-Up page, where you'll find various payment methods and instructions.


How is the pricing calculated for print jobs?

The pricing depends on factors like print type (color or black & white), paper size, and quantity. For more details, visit our Services page.
